Renewable Energy

Every plant is being converted to on-site solar, wind offtake, or certified renewable grid supply — no exceptions for older sites.

  • Target: 100% renewable electricity by 2030
  • 8 of 11 plants already converted
  • On-site solar live at 4 sites
78%of 95%

Packaging Circularity

Every new SKU is designed against a recyclability checklist before it reaches the production line, not after.

  • Target: 95% recyclable or reusable by 2028
  • 78% of packaging qualifies today
  • 12,400 tonnes of virgin plastic removed since 2021
-30%of -50%

Water Intensity

Closed-loop treatment and reuse systems are rolling out first at our water-stressed plants, then everywhere else.

  • Target: -50% water intensity by 2030
  • -30% achieved against 2019 baseline
  • Closed-loop treatment live at 6 sites
-27%of -50%

Carbon Emissions

Scope 1 and 2 emissions are falling as renewable conversion scales, with logistics routing next on the list.

  • Target: -50% Scope 1&2 emissions by 2030
  • -27% achieved against 2019 baseline
  • 184,000 tonnes CO₂e avoided last year
92%of 100%

Zero Waste to Landfill

Production waste is sorted, composted or resold as feedstock before it ever reaches a landfill truck.

  • Target: 100% diversion by 2027
  • 92% of production waste diverted today
  • 7 plants already at zero-waste-to-landfill

The Journey

From baseline to 2030, one milestone at a time.

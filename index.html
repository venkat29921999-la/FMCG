How It's Made

From raw material to retail-ready, in four stages.

Every SKU follows the same audited pathway — because consistency is the product as much as the formula is.

01

Source

Raw materials are traced to certified farms and mills, with every batch logged before it enters our supply chain.

02

Formulate

In-house labs test each formulation against safety, performance and shelf-stability benchmarks before approval.

03

Produce

Eleven plants run under one quality standard, with real-time monitoring on every production line.

04

Distribute

Cold chain and ambient logistics move finished goods to retail partners in under 48 hours domestically.
